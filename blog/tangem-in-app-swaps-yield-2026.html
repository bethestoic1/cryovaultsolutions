The Exchange Problem: KYC, Fees, and Hidden Risk

What Happens When You Swap on an Exchange

Step 1: Create account. Upload ID. Passport selfie. Proof of address. Hours of verification.

Step 2: They hold your funds in their wallet. You have an IOU.

Step 3: Swap. They take a cut from the spread (often hidden in the price).

Step 4: Withdraw. Subject to limits, withdrawal queues, and "maintenance windows."

Step 5: Your data is theirs. Today it's regulatory compliance. Tomorrow it's a data breach.

The real cost: Privacy gone. Data sold. Control surrendered. Risk concentrated in one company.
