The Technology: EIP-7702 & Why It Matters

If you're curious about how this works under the hood:

The problem solved: Ethereum traditionally has two account types:

  • EOA (Externally Owned Account): Controlled by your private key. Can hold money, sign transactions. But can't execute smart contract logic.
  • Smart Contract Account: Can execute arbitrary code. But requires migration to a new address (you lose your original address).

The old workarounds were all broken:

  • Account abstraction (ERC-4337): You had to migrate to a new wallet address. Not practical for most users.
  • Meta-transactions: Relied on other services trusting you. Centralization risk.
  • Centralized relayers: Someone else paying for your gas. Privacy and censorship risks.

EIP-7702 (Pectra upgrade, May 2025): Lets your account temporarily attach smart contract logic WITHOUT changing your address. You sign a message (zero cost), and Tangem's contract handles the fee conversion. You stay at your original address. Your funds stay where they are.
