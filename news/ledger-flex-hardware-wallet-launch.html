Ledger Flex: A New Standard in Hardware Security and Clarity

2026-03-26 · security

Ledger has officially launched its newest device: the Ledger Flex. This device sits between the Nano and the Stax, offering a large, secure E-ink touchscreen that allows users to verify transactions with unprecedented clarity while maintaining the highest level of hardware security.

Secure Display Technology

The Flex features a 'Secure Display' that is directly controlled by the ST33 secure element. This ensures that what you see on the screen is exactly what you are signing, eliminating 'blind signing' risks that are prevalent in software-based wallets.

NFC and Mobile Integration

With built-in NFC, the Ledger Flex allows for seamless tap-to-sign functionality with mobile dApps. This combination of convenience and cold-storage security is designed to drive mass adoption of hardware-secured digital asset management.
