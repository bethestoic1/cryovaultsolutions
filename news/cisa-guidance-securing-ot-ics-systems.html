CISA & Partners Release Guidance on Securing OT/ICS Systems

2026-03-26 · security

The Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA), in collaboration with the NSA and international partners, has issued a comprehensive advisory on securing Operational Technology (OT) and Industrial Control Systems (ICS). This guidance is critical for operators of hardware-secured environments.

Core Recommendations

The report emphasizes the need for physical isolation of critical OT networks from the public internet. It recommends the use of hardware security modules (HSMs) and multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative access to control systems.

Implementation for Safe Custody

For institutions managing digital assets or critical data vaults, these CISA standards provide a blueprint for 'Zero Trust' hardware architecture. This includes regular firmware audits and the use of air-gapped signing devices to prevent remote exploitation.
