Cryptographically Verifiable Cold Storage

Moving beyond simple backups: cryptographically verifiable retention gives you proof that vaulted data is unchanged and recoverable. Our 2026 Cryo-Airgap protocols ensure zero-data-drift and provable integrity.

Why verification matters

Traditional backups can fail silently—corruption, misconfiguration, or drift over time. For regulated and high-value data, you need:

  • Integrity proof — Evidence that each object or volume has not been altered since write.
  • Zero-data-drift — Guarantees that what you restore matches what was vaulted.
  • Recoverability evidence — Demonstrated ability to restore within required windows (e.g. under 120 minutes for SEC/NIS2).
